In this example, the fill() method was used. The fill() method uses constant values or arrays to fill arrays or specific channels. When the color values and channels are arrays of the same length, a one-to-one correspondence between colors and channels can be achieved. The fill() method is an in place operation. ImageArray and Img have the same purpose.
